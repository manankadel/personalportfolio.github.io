A little about me

My name is Manan Kadel and I am an undergraduate currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from SRM University. I am constantly striving to become the best version of myself in everything I do. Whether it's academics, extracurricular activities, or personal pursuits.

I do amazing things for clients

GRAPHIC DESIGNING

Over the past two years, I have been practising and developing my skills in graphic designing. Whether I'm designing a logo, website, or mobile application, I strive to create designs that are both visually striking and easy to navigate.

WEBSITE DEVELOPER

As a developer, I understand the importance of creating websites that not only look great but also provide a seamless user experience. I am well-versed in using a variety of programming languages which allow me to create dynamic and responsive websites that can adapt to any screen size or device.

VIDEO EDITOR

As a video editor, I am passionate about creating compelling visual content that captures the essence of a story and engages viewers. I have developed my skills over time by working on various projects, from short films to documentaries, commercials, and music videos.
